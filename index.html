About Us

Beautiful Story

Delicia bakery was set up in Bandung, Indonesia in 2014 and we are a genuine privately-owned company with six ages of experience. We offer our clients a wide scope of breads, cakes, pastries and cookies. We take pride in using natural and high quality ingredients in our bakeries.

Our range of products has diversified and expanded to meet the needs of the growing customer base. Our vision is to be a company that provides high quality of bakery products with great taste and excellence to customers. Our mission is to prioritize innovation and variation of products and provide easiness and comfort of customer services.
